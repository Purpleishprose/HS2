Highland Staffs


Jason is a lover of the outdoors, and spends his free time in the mountains around Asheville, North Carolina with his pug, Alice, and mountain feist, Dixie. With a knack for botony, Jason began wood working over 10 years ago, making coffee tables and other custom pieces. He makes each staff by hand, focusing on maintaining the uniqueness of each and every one.
